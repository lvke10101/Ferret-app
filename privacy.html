01

Who This Covers

This Policy applies to anyone using the GradeVault app or web app (together, the "Platform"), operated as an independent student utility — not affiliated with any university. It should be read alongside the Terms and Conditions. GradeVault is the data controller for the personal data described below, under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 ("NDPA").

02

Data We Collect

Only what the Platform needs to function. No location tracking, no advertising identifiers, no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs, no third-party trackers.

CategoryWhat
AccountEmail address and password (handled by our authentication provider; GradeVault never sees or stores your raw password).
ProfileNickname, department, level, admission year, and an avatar you pick from a fixed set of built-in options — not a photo you upload.
Academic self-entriesCourses and grades you enter yourself to calculate your GPA. This is self-reported; GradeVault does not verify it against any institution's records.
ContentPosts, comments, reports you submit, and bookmarks/reactions.
CreditsYour credit balance and transaction history within the Platform. Credit purchases are arranged manually over WhatsApp with an administrator — GradeVault does not collect or store payment card, bank, or transfer details.
Push notificationsA device token (via Google Firebase Cloud Messaging) if you enable notifications, so we can deliver them. No token, no push.
TechnicalStandard request logs kept by our hosting/database provider (timestamps, IP address) for security and abuse prevention.
03

Why We Collect It

  • To create and secure your account, and show you catalogs, posts, and course material scoped to your department, level, and semester.
  • To calculate and display your GPA from the courses and grades you enter.
  • To operate the leaderboard, notifications, comments, and credit system.
  • To review reported Content and enforce the Terms.
  • To keep the Platform secure and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

04

Lawful Basis

Under Section 25 of the NDPA, we process your data on the basis of the consent you give at registration, and where necessary for the performance of your account relationship with GradeVault — for example, storing the courses you enter so your GPA renders correctly. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 07); doing so may mean the Platform can no longer provide the account-dependent features that consent supports.

05

Who Sees Your Data

  • Other users see what you choose to make visible: your nickname, avatar, department, posts, and comments. Your GPA and course entries are private to your account.
  • Supabase, our hosting and database provider, stores your data on our behalf under a standard processing arrangement.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging receives your device token solely to deliver push notifications, if enabled.
  • GradeVault administrators can access reported Content and credit records to moderate the Platform and process manual top-ups.
We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any party for marketing purposes, and we do not sell it.
06

Retention and Deletion

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile and academic entries are removed; comments and posts may be retained in de-identified or moderated form where needed to preserve the integrity of threads other users have engaged with, or where retention is required by law (including data-retention obligations under the Cybercrimes (Amendment) Act 2024).

07

Your Rights

Under Sections 34–38 of the NDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent to processing.
  • Object to processing.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these, contact [privacy contact — coming soon].

08

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards — encrypted storage, row-level access controls, and restricted administrator access. In the event of a personal data breach likely to result in risk to you, we will notify affected users and, where required, the Nigeria Data Protection Commission, in accordance with Section 40 of the NDPA. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

09

Cross-Border Storage

Your data may be stored on servers located outside Nigeria via our hosting/database provider. Where this occurs, GradeVault relies on that provider's standard contractual and security safeguards to keep a level of protection consistent with the NDPA.

10

Local Storage on Your Device

The Platform stores your session token on your device (via standard browser/WebView local storage) so you stay signed in between visits. This is functional, not tracking — we don't use it to build an advertising profile, and no third-party analytics or advertising cookies are set.

11

Children

GradeVault is not directed at children under 18. Where a minor is permitted to register, additional consent safeguards under Section 31 of the NDPA apply, and we restrict processing of that minor's sensitive data accordingly.
